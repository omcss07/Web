ME

In an age where technology shapes our daily lives and interactions, I am driven by a vision of a society that can engage with these advancements thoughtfully and ethically. My project aims to provide a platform for individuals to share news and express opinions about the societal impacts of technology, particularly in areas like artificial intelligence, justice, and human behavior.

Inspired by the works of Yuval Noah Harari, Ray Bradbury, and Aldous Huxley, I initiated this project with the hope of changing perspectives and creating a vision of a harmonious society. My goal is to foster collaboration and understanding, rather than division and destruction, as we navigate the complexities of our technological landscape together
